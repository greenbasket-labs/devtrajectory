DevTrajectory

Builder signals over time.

Problem

Years of experience do not always reflect real-world impact. Some developers quietly ship value early, while others accumulate time without meaningful delivery.

Today, developer evaluation relies heavily on resumes, titles, or self-reported claims rather than observable builder signals.

Idea

DevTrajectory explores a signal-based view of developer growth. Instead of rankings or titles, it focuses on publicly observable builder activity over time.

The goal is not to judge developers, but to make contribution and progression visible in a transparent, neutral way.

Builder Signals (Examples)

  • Deployed smart contracts
  • Applications with real users
  • Open-source contributions
  • Hackathon submissions
  • Protocol or infrastructure integrations
